About Us

At QScript, we take pride in sourcing the finest beans from around the globe, ensuring each cup boasts a rich and robust flavor profile that tantalizes the taste buds. Whether it's a velvety latte, a bold espresso, or a delicate pour-over, our skilled baristas craft each beverage with precision and care, elevating the coffee-drinking experience to new heights.

Beyond our dedication to exceptional coffee, QScript also offers a delectable selection of freshly baked pastries and light bites, perfect for pairing with your favorite brew or enjoying on their own. From flaky croissants to indulgent chocolate chip cookies, each treat is lovingly prepared in-house, using only the finest ingredients.

But QScript is more than just a coffee shop; it's a community hub where friendships are forged and ideas are exchanged. With ample seating and free Wi-Fi, it's a popular spot for students to study, professionals to work remotely, and friends to catch up over a steaming cup of coffee.

Menu

Espresso

A concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely ground coffee beans.

Cappuccino

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, resulting in a creamy and rich coffee experience.

Latte

A coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. It is typically creamier and milder in taste compared to other coffee beverages.

Croissant

A buttery, flaky pastry made from layered dough. Perfectly paired with a cup of coffee.

Mocha

A delightful fusion of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, creating a harmonious blend of rich coffee flavor with a hint of sweetness.

Macchiato

An espresso "stained" with a dollop of frothy milk, balancing the intensity of the espresso with a touch of creamy texture.

Scone

Tender, crumbly pastries studded with fruits or nuts, ideal for pairing with a cup of coffee. Their slightly sweet flavor and hearty texture make them a comforting treat any time of day.

Coffee Cake

Moist and flavorful cake infused with rich coffee flavor and topped with a buttery crumb streusel, creating a delightful balance of sweetness and warmth.
